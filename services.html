We design trust frameworks that protect what you have built from creditors, probate delays, and unnecessary tax exposure while keeping control exactly where it belongs. If you have children, a business, or assets worth protecting, this conversation belongs in your strategy.

Retirement Income

Annuities and Retirement Income Strategy

An annuity is an insurance product. Not an investment. That distinction matters enormously when you understand what it is designed to do: provide guaranteed income you cannot outlive, structured to complement and extend what IBC builds.

When IBC establishes your private banking system and an annuity locks in a guaranteed income floor, the result is a retirement built on certainty rather than market performance. We use both instruments together because that is how the wealthy plan retirement. Not picking stocks. Building income floors with insurance and letting everything above that compound freely.

  • ✓ Guaranteed income you cannot outlive regardless of market conditions
  • ✓ Tax deferred growth that fits within your broader tax mitigation strategy
  • ✓ Structured to complement IBC. Not compete with it.
  • ✓ No market exposure. Your income floor does not fluctuate.
  • ✓ Positioned inside trust and estate structures for seamless legacy planning
