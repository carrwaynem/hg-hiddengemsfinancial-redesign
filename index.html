The Process

From First Conversation to Custom Strategy

A proven process designed to educate first and build second so you fully understand and own the strategy we create together.

1

Pre Education

Before we meet, you receive foundational video content that reframes how you think about money, cash flow, and what financial strategy actually looks like. You come to the first call ready.

2

Discovery Call — Full Cash Flow Mapping

We map how money comes in, where it goes, and what it could be doing. We revisit the Infinite Banking concept in the context of your actual situation and cast a vision for what the strategy looks like.

3

Written Overview Delivered

You receive a written overview of everything we discussed so you can review it at your own pace, do your own diligence, and prepare questions for meeting two.

4

Strategy Presentation

We walk through your custom policy illustration in detail, adjusting as needed, answering every question, and bringing in referral partners when the situation calls for it. You make an informed decision. No pressure.

5

Ongoing Relationship

Access to Wayne does not end at issuance. As your financial picture evolves — income changes, family grows, business scales — the strategy evolves with it. You are not a closed file. You are a client.
